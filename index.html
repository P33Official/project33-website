07 / FAQ

Direct answers.

What is Project 33?

Project 33 is a community-powered build lab intended to turn selected ideas into useful releases through focused, transparent 33-day missions.

What is P33?

P33 is the proposed participation token for the Project 33 ecosystem. Its intended functions include mission access, structured community input, verified-contribution recognition, and useful community benefits.

Why does Project 33 need a token?

The working concept is that P33 will provide a consistent participation layer across missions. The team will not launch it unless meaningful functions can be delivered and the structure passes legal, technical, security, and operational review.

What is Mission 000?

Mission 000 is the current pre-countdown foundation phase. The team is being assembled, responsibilities are being defined, and P33’s purpose, architecture, controls, and readiness requirements are being developed.

What happens after P33 launches?

Mission 001 is intended to launch P33. Future missions are intended to use the Project 33 ecosystem to help produce tools, resources, open-source projects, products, experiences, or other useful releases.

Can I buy P33 now?

No. P33 has not been deployed or launched. Any token currently claiming to be P33 is unofficial.

Does P33 represent ownership or guaranteed profit?

No. P33 is not intended to represent equity, ownership, revenue sharing, guaranteed passive income, guaranteed appreciation, guaranteed liquidity, or guaranteed exchange listings.

Where will official information be posted?

Use only project33.tech, @P33Official on X, and the official Project 33 Discord. The final contract address will be published consistently across all three sources.
