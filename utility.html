EARLIER P33 UTILITY CONCEPT

This page preserves a previous planning direction for transparency. It is not the current P33 launch model.

Archived: July 19, 2026Superseded concept

On this page

Archive noticeWhat changedCurrent concept
No longer currentThe earlier utility-first model described P33 as a participation token for 33-day build missions. Project 33 later chose a simpler Five-Seal community mystery framework.

What changed

The earlier concept proposed mission access, token-based community input, contribution recognition, and holder benefits. Those ideas are not part of the current launch promise and should not be treated as planned P33 features.

The change was made to keep the project understandable, achievable, and honest about what is actually being created.

The current P33 concept

P33 is an experimental community mystery token connected to five public milestone-driven Seals. Each milestone must remain verified for 33 seconds before its Seal opens permanently.

Future features, products, rewards, or utility may be explored later, but they will not be advertised as commitments before they exist.
