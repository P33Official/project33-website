COMMON QUESTIONS

The important details

Why use a 33-second verification?

It prevents a single instant market-cap spike from opening a seal while keeping the moment fast, visible, and fun for the community.

Do I need to own P33 to follow the mystery?

No. The unlocked reveals and public investigation are intended to be viewable by the community. Participation in discussion does not require a purchase.

Will the milestones be secret?

No. The unlock levels will be announced publicly through project33.tech and official Project 33 channels.

Can an opened seal close again?

No. Once the 33-second verification completes, that seal is permanently unlocked.

Are the milestones instructions to buy or hold?

No. They are entertainment triggers for a community mystery. Project 33 does not ask participants to coordinate purchases or maintain a market-cap level.

Follow the mystery—not promises of profit.

P33 is experimental and may be highly volatile. Market cap is not project cash, guaranteed value, or proof of future performance. Nothing on this page is financial, legal, or tax advice.
