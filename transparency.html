Public log

July 19, 2026

Official X account made more prominent

Direct links to the official Project 33 X account were added to the homepage navigation, homepage hero, Five Seals page, and Trust Center navigation: https://x.com/P33Official. Community members should verify that announcements come from @P33Official.

July 19, 2026

Official Discord invite published

The permanent Project 33 Discord invite was added to the public website and Official Links page: https://discord.gg/6aAxzhkwvJ. Community members should use only the invite published through project33.tech or another verified Project 33 channel.

July 19, 2026

All five Seal experiences completed and privately validated

Production is complete for The Signal, The Black File, The Choice, The Key, and The Door. Public activation packages, private production masters, supporting artwork, community announcement copy, and post-unlock website states have been prepared and validated. This production milestone does not mean that P33 has launched or that any Seal is publicly open.

July 19, 2026

Seal 05 — The Door completed

The Chapter One finale was produced and privately validated, including Fragment 05 — The Flame, the completed Project 33 Mark, a Final Transmission running exactly 1 minute 33 seconds, the permanent Chapter One Archive, and an optional community-recognition framework. Separate activation packages preserve either possible Seal 03 result.

July 19, 2026

Seal 04 — The Key completed

The browser-based assembly experience was completed and privately validated. It combines the first three fragments, reveals Fragment 04 — The Key, opens the P33 Community Archive, and prepares the Final Seal condition. Branch-safe activation packages were created for either Seal 03 outcome.

July 19, 2026

Seal 03 — The Choice completed

The Choice Protocol was completed and privately validated, including the Red Door and White Door previews, a planned 24-hour Discord vote, Fragment 03 — The Split, and separate result packages for either winning path. The public voting package contains neither unreleased transmission.

July 19, 2026

Seal 02 — The Black File completed

The interactive browser dossier was completed and privately validated, including five file sections, three recoverable redactions, a phrase reconstruction terminal, Fragment 02 — The Thread, and a hidden annex that introduces the two Choice paths. Puzzle answers and private production notes remain outside the public website.

July 19, 2026

Seal 01 produced and held for activation

Seal 01 — The Signal has been produced and privately validated, including its 33-second transmission, first glyph fragment, clue interaction, and Black File teaser. Reveal assets remain outside the public pre-launch website and will not be published before the corresponding milestone is officially verified.

July 19, 2026

Chapter One story and canonical Seal lineup finalized

The connected Five-Seal narrative was finalized as The Signal, The Black File, The Choice, The Key, and The Door. Each reveal contributes one fragment to the complete Project 33 Mark, culminating in the community becoming the missing final fragment.

July 19, 2026

Founding 333 launch gate adopted

Project 33 will remain in Pre-Launch until the official Discord reaches 333 Founding Members. At that threshold the public website changes to Launch State, allowing official launch activation to begin. Reaching the threshold does not automatically deploy P33, begin trading, or open a Seal; those events require separate official announcements.

July 19, 2026

Website aligned to the Five-Seal concept

The public website was revised so the homepage, principles, status pages, terms, risk language, navigation, metadata, and footer consistently describe P33 as a community mystery built around five milestone-driven Seals. The previous utility-first and 33-day build-lab concept was removed from current-facing pages and preserved as a clearly labeled historical archive.

July 19, 2026

Community Seals explainer added

A dedicated Seals page was added to explain the experience in simple language. It introduces the five planned reveals, the 33-second verification rule, timer resets, data interruptions, permanent unlocks, and the fact that no purchase is required to view public reveals or participate in the public mystery.

July 19, 2026

Milestone Engine v0.1 completed in simulation mode

A Cloudflare-ready milestone engine was created to manage five configurable Seals, a 33-second verification timer, resets, data pauses, permanent unlocks, automatic progression, protected administrative controls, and a public event log. Local validation completed with all automated and end-to-end checks passing. The engine is not yet deployed to production and is not connected to live token data.

July 19, 2026

Thirty-three-second milestone verification adopted

Each announced milestone must remain at or above its threshold for 33 consecutive seconds before the corresponding Seal opens. A confirmed reading below the threshold resets the timer. A verified market-data interruption pauses the process instead of forcing a reset. Once opened, a Seal remains open permanently.

July 19, 2026

Five-Seal community mystery framework adopted

Project 33 initially simplified its launch concept into a five-reveal community mystery using the working names The Signal, The Black File, The Choice, The Drop, and The Final Reveal. Those provisional names were later refined into the canonical lineup recorded above: The Signal, The Black File, The Choice, The Key, and The Door.

July 19, 2026

Live Founding Member counter connected

The website’s Founding Member count was connected to the official Discord role count through a restricted Cloudflare Worker. The counter is informational and does not grant access, transfer roles, connect wallets, or perform token-related actions.

July 18, 2026

Working project purpose and P33 utility model adopted

Project 33 was defined as a community-powered build lab using focused 33-day missions to produce useful releases. P33 was defined as a proposed participation token supporting mission access, structured community input, contribution recognition, and deliverable community benefits. This utility-first concept was later superseded by the simpler Five-Seal community mystery framework recorded above.

July 18, 2026

Trust Center added

Project Principles, Risk Disclosure, Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, Official Links & Anti-Scam guidance, and this Transparency Log were prepared for publication. Token-specific legal terms remain subject to qualified legal review.

July 18, 2026

Project website published

The official Project 33 website was published at project33.tech. The website states that the P33 contract is not deployed, trading is not live, and the public launch countdown has not started.

July 18, 2026

Simplified mission adopted

Project 33’s immediate objective was defined as building and launching P33 transparently through a structured campaign. The first 333 community members remain a supporting milestone, not the primary mission.

Future disclosures

Material updates expected before launch include:

  • Selected blockchain network and decision rationale.
  • Official market-data provider, tracked market or pair, and fallback rules.
  • Final milestone thresholds and activation of the live 33-second verification system.
  • Token purpose, supply, allocations, vesting, liquidity, creator fees, and treasury controls.
  • Smart-contract source code, permissions, ownership status, and verification details.
  • Security reviews, known limitations, and material findings.
  • Official deployment transaction and verified contract address.
  • Material incidents, data-provider failures, launch delays, or changes to the launch plan.

The contents of unopened Seals may remain intentionally undisclosed until their corresponding milestones are verified.
