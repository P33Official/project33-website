Project 33 will never ask for

  • Your seed phrase, recovery phrase, private key, or wallet password.
  • Remote access to your computer or phone.
  • An advance payment to “protect,” “unlock,” or “verify” your funds.
  • Crypto sent to a personal wallet through an unsolicited direct message.
  • A payment in exchange for guaranteed profits, guaranteed allocation, or guaranteed returns.

Project 33 will not privately deploy a different contract for selected users. A legitimate final contract address must match across the website, official X account, and official Discord.

Verification checklist

  1. Type project33.tech directly into your browser or use a trusted bookmark.
  2. Confirm the X username is exactly @P33Official.
  3. Use the Discord invite linked from the official website.
  4. Confirm the full contract address character by character on all official sources.
  5. Never connect a wallet or sign a transaction because of urgency, fear, or a direct message.
  6. Read the wallet prompt and approval details before signing.

Report suspected impersonation or scams

Report impersonating accounts using the reporting tools on the platform where they appear. Notify the Project 33 community through an official channel without posting private keys, seed phrases, or other sensitive information.

  • Report fraud to the U.S. Federal Trade Commission
  • Investor.gov fraud-prevention resources
