08 / FAQ

Direct answers.

What is Project 33?

Project 33 is the creator of P33 and the Five-Seal community mystery.

What is P33?

P33 is the experimental community token connected to the Five-Seal experience. It does not represent equity, ownership, revenue sharing, or guaranteed financial benefit.

What happens at 333 Founding Members?

Project 33 changes from Pre-Launch to Launch State. That completes the community launch gate and allows official launch activation to begin, but it does not automatically deploy P33, start trading, or open a Seal.

What are the five Seals?

The Signal, The Black File, The Choice, The Key, and The Door. Each opens only after launch when its public milestone completes the 33-second verification.

How does the 33-second verification work?

When the next milestone is reached, the automated timer starts. P33 must remain at or above that threshold for 33 consecutive seconds before the Seal opens.

What happens if market cap drops during verification?

A valid reading below the milestone resets the timer to 33 seconds.

What happens if market data becomes unavailable?

The verification pauses rather than resetting. It resumes after valid data returns.

Do I need to buy P33 to participate?

No. Anyone can view public reveals, follow clues, join community discussions, and participate in public mystery activities.

Can I buy P33 now?

No. P33 has not launched. Any token currently claiming to be the official P33 is unofficial.

Are the milestones instructions to buy or hold?

No. They are entertainment triggers. Project 33 does not ask participants to coordinate purchases or maintain a market-cap level.

Where will official information be posted?

Use only project33.tech, @P33Official on X, and the official Discord linked from this website. The final contract address will be published consistently across all three.
