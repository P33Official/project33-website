PROJECT PRINCIPLES

The commitments guiding Project 33 before, during, and after the Five-Seal P33 community mystery.

Updated: July 19, 2026 Pre-launch version 1.1

On this page

The standardEight principlesAccountability
Public commitment These principles are intended to be measurable. When Project 33 changes direction, the change and its reason should be recorded publicly.

The Project 33 standard

Say what it is. Do not invent what it is not. Project 33 will prioritize clear rules, honest descriptions, public status updates, and responsible execution over manufactured urgency or unsupported promises.

01

Build in public

Meaningful milestones, decisions, concept changes, revisions, and setbacks will be documented instead of hidden.

02

Safety over hype

The mystery and its milestones do not justify unfinished systems, misleading urgency, or unsafe technical decisions.

03

No guaranteed returns

Project 33 will not promise profit, price appreciation, passive income, liquidity, or exchange listings.

04

Verify before trust

The official contract address and launch information will be published consistently on the website, X account, and Discord.

05

Disclose control

Material token controls, creator fees, allocations, liquidity arrangements, and administrative permissions will be explained before launch.

06

Respect the community

Questions and criticism may be moderated for safety and conduct, but not merely because they are inconvenient.

07

No fabricated credibility

Project 33 will not use fake endorsements, false partnerships, deceptive scarcity, or misleading claims of approval.

08

Do not invent utility

P33 will be described honestly as a community mystery token. Features, rewards, or future products will not be advertised unless they are real and ready to deliver.

09

No coordinated trading

Milestones are entertainment triggers, not requests to buy, hold, defend, or manipulate a market-cap level.

10

Report incidents honestly

Material security incidents, tracker failures, data problems, or launch disruptions will be acknowledged with known facts and corrective actions.

Accountability

These principles do not eliminate risk. They define the standard against which Project 33’s actions can be evaluated. The Transparency Log will record material decisions and status changes.
